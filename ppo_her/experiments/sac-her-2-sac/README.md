# sac-her-2-sac

Turn off HER after reward of 0 is reached.  Results not shown in [Maximum Entropy HER (MEHER)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.24016).  This would correspond to Red line in Figures 6 and 7 of [Maximum Entropy HER (MEHER)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.24016), except that this uses SAC instead of PPO.
